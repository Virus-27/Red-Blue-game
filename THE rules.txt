Easy rules for RED-BLUE game:
-2 players, one creates the game ,the other joins

Objective? WIN , obviously

Game playthrough:

-10 rounds

-each round ,each player chooses between two colors, RED or BLUE (name of the game)

-depending on each which color were picked ,there are RUUUULES how many points everyone gets:
  -if both players picked RED ,both will get 3 points (Happy ending)
  -if both players picked BLUE ,both will lose 3 points (Bad ending)
  -if one players picked RED and the other BLUE, the one who picked BLUE will get 6 points ,while the other will lose 6 points (Betrayal ending)

After round 4 ,players are ask if they want to discuss; if both say YES ,a chat box will be opened, if one says NO , no more chat box. 

During the discussion ,each can stop the discussion whenever they want by pressing "STOP" button , which will close the chat ,hiding it ,so none could read it.

Same thing will happen after round 8, but now it more serious cuz the points are double for the rounds 9 and 10!!!

Winning condition:

-players who have positive score are the winners
-players who have negative score are losers


Additional features:
-every player can withdraw anytime ,automatically losing the game
-administrator page where u can see all the games ,and able to sort by date, filter by game state (ongoing, finalized, abandoned, win, lose), and see choice history of the game
